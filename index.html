Getting started

From sign-up to your first rent record in minutes.

STEP 1

Add your properties

Create a profile for each property — apartments, plazas, or single units — with the number of stalls or units it has.

STEP 2

Add tenants & units

List who's renting what, their contact details, move-in date and monthly rent. Mark empty units as vacant.

STEP 3

Record payments

Each month, log who's paid. NyumbaTrack tracks who's due, sends notifications, and resets automatically.
