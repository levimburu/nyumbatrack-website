Everything in one place

Stop juggling three apps
to manage one property.

NyumbaTrack is the single system that keeps every property, tenant, and shilling organised — without the complexity.

Tenant Records

Every tenant's name, phone, unit, rent amount, move-in date, and deposit — stored, searchable, and always up to date.

Payments and Receipts

Record M-Pesa, cash, or bank transfers. Generate a branded PDF receipt in one tap — no printer, no forms.

Units Overview

See every unit at a glance — occupied, vacant, or overdue. Know exactly what each unit is earning each month.

Live Notifications

You and your agents are notified instantly when rent is recorded, a tenant is added, or a unit changes status.

Deposit Tracking

See exactly how much is held per tenant. Mark deposits as refunded automatically when a tenant moves out.

Arrears Tracking

Not just "unpaid" — the real number of months owed, per tenant, updated every time a payment is recorded or missed.

Agent Accounts

Invite a caretaker or secretary to manage a specific property — without giving them access to your whole portfolio.

Multi-property Dashboard

Own multiple buildings? Switch between them in one tap. Your entire portfolio in one login, one screen.

Monthly Reports

See total collected, outstanding, and per-property breakdowns — every month, automatically. No spreadsheets needed.

Getting started

Set up in under
ten minutes.

No training, no setup calls. Add your property, add your tenants, and start recording rent today.

01

Create your account

Sign up with your phone number or email. No lengthy onboarding, no credit card required. You're in the dashboard in under a minute.

02

Add your properties and tenants

Add each property, then fill in your tenants — their unit, rent amount, move-in date, and deposit. Takes about five minutes per building.

03

Record rent every month

Each month, mark who paid, who paid partially, and who hasn't paid. NyumbaTrack handles the maths, the arrears, and the receipts automatically.
