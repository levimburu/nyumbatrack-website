Everything in one place

Stop managing rentals from three different apps.

NyumbaTrack is the single system that keeps every property, tenant, and shilling organised.

Tenant Records

Every tenant's details — phone, email, move-in date, rent, deposit — organised by unit and instantly searchable.

Payments & Receipts

Record M-Pesa, cash, or bank payments. Generate a professional PDF receipt in one tap, branded under your company.

Units Overview

See every unit at a glance — occupied or vacant — with the expected monthly rent per unit.

Deposit Tracking

Know exactly how much is held for each tenant. Mark deposits as refunded when a tenant moves out.

Live Notifications

Landlords and agents are notified instantly when rent is recorded, a tenant moves in, or a unit changes.

Arrears Tracking

See exactly how many months each tenant is behind — not a vague "unpaid" label, but the real number owed.

Getting started

From sign-up to your first rent record in minutes.

01

Add your properties

Create a profile for each property — whether it's a 4-unit block or a 100-stall commercial plaza. Set the total units and location.

02

Register your tenants

Add each tenant with their unit, rent amount, move-in date, and deposit. Vacant units are tracked separately with their expected rent.

03

Record payments monthly

Each month, record who paid in full, who paid partially, and who topped up. The system tracks arrears and updates statuses automatically.

Two roles, one system

Built for landlords — and the agents who manage day to day.

Invite your caretaker or secretary to manage a specific property without giving them access to everything else.

Landlord

Full visibility, full control

  • View every property, tenant, and payment in one dashboard
  • Invite agents to specific properties only with a one-time code
  • Get notified instantly when rent is paid or a tenant is added
  • See which tenants are behind and by how many months
  • Access year-to-date collection reports anytime
Agent / Caretaker

Manage operations, nothing more

  • Add tenants and record payments for assigned properties only
  • View deposits, units, and tenant statuses
  • Cannot edit or delete properties
  • Joins via a one-time invite code from the landlord
  • Receives real-time notifications for their assigned properties
